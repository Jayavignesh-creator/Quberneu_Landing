Webinar: Making the move to Dynamics 365 Business Central: all you need to know

Written by Codec | Dec 3, 2024 4:18:00 PM

Guided by seasoned industry experts this webinar will delve into the key features and benefits of D365 BC and demonstrate how the use of Copilot can greatly enhance your productivity on a daily basis with such tasks as bank reconciliations, predicting late payments and cashflow forecasting.  It will also outline how to migrate to the cloud from an on-premise solution such as Dynamics GP and how the use of our GP BC accelerators can make this a smoother and more effective process. 

 

Webinar highlights include:

  • Overview and demonstration of Dynamics Business Central – main functions and advantages, including how Copilot in BC can assist with various tasks
  • Connection of Dynamics BC with the Microsoft environment – including teams integration for teamwork and fast response times
  • How to move to Dynamics BC from an on prem application (such as GP) – using accelerators to enhance the process
  • Supporting your Business Central environment
