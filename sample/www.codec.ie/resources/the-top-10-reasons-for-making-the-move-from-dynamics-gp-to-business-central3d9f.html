Top 10 reasons for making the move from Dynamics GP to Business Central

Written by Codec | Mar 24, 2021 1:00:00 AM

If the past year has taught us anything it’s that no-one can predict the future!  The much-overused phrase the “new normal” has become the de-facto way of describing a new way of living and working and how we interact with people daily. It means digital transformation and the increased use of technology to help companies achieve more.

As organisations continue to work in this new environment, many have begun to explore options around moving their applications to the cloud.

It’s no different for ERP systems and there is a drive to migrate many existing Dynamics GP users to Dynamics 365 Business Central.

Whilst existing Dynamics GP users are familiar with the technology and what it can deliver for them on a daily basis, there are many compelling reasons as to why you should consider making the move to Business Central in the future. We will outline some of these key reasons below:

Benefits of moving from GP to Business Central Online

  • Data migration toolkit available to move Dynamics GP data and transaction history to Business Central.
  • Full integration with Microsoft 365 (Outlook, Word, Excel, etc.), Teams, SharePoint and the Power Platform.
  • 24/7 access from anywhere, on any device.
  • Fully scalable SaaS, with option to deploy on-premises in Azure
  • Lower IT costs
  • Flexible and easy to customise and upgrade and easier integration with external applications
  • Includes CRM and better manufacturing and distribution capabilities than GP
  • Provides better support for branches and multiple entities than GP.
  • Extendable functionality through Microsoft AppSource
  • Out-of-the-box dashboards, approval workflows and extensive financial reporting options

GP to Business Central Migration Triggers

So, what are the triggers that companies often identify with to pre-empt a move to Dynamics Business Central from GP?  Typically, in making a move to Business Central, some of the following scenarios may form part of the consideration process:

  • Dynamics GP is too expensive on a yearly basis.
  • You want to reduce cost and/or risk by hosting your data and application in the cloud.
  • Any critical business process for which you currently use a 3rd-party add-on has an equivalent Dynamics 365 app available in AppSource
  • There is a particular difficulty in GP or a feature in BC that makes you want to switch.

There are many compelling reasons why companies should consider making the move from Dynamics GP to Dynamics Business Central sooner rather than later.
