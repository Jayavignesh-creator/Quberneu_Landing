Making the move to Dynamics Business Central: all you need to know

Written by Codec | May 10, 2021 12:00:00 AM

This webinar focused on learning about the benefits of transitioning to Dynamics 365 Business Central, how best to make the move and what resources are available in doing so. It also addressed how other companies are managing the move and what impact it is having on their day to day operations.

Agenda:

  • Why Make the Move to Dynamics 365 Business Central - Key challenges and benefits

  • Dynamics 365 Business Central Overview and interactive demonstration (including direct integration with MS Outlook and Exel)

  • Customer Success Story: Hear how one company have successfully implemented Business Central online and how it has transformed their day-to-day operations

  • Licensing requirements, Special offers and next steps

  • Q& A
