Microsoft Fabric: how to build a robust data platform and generate AI insights

Written by Codec | Sep 26, 2024 3:41:20 PM

Whether you’re looking to implement cutting edge AI solutions or simply want to manage your data more effectively, the starting point must be a robust data platform.

This on demand webinar was hosted by Agustina Lazzarini, Azure Data & AI Solution Architect from Codec in conjunction with Paul Norris, Senior Data & AI Solutions sales specialist with Microsoft.  It also featured Sorcha O'Callaghan, Head of Data and Analytics from the Department of Transport who discussed their experience of implementing Microsoft Fabric in their environment.


 

Webinar highlights include:

  • The basics of Microsoft Fabric and how it enables “one view” of your data
  • The importance of building a robust data platform 
  • How Fabric streamlines your efforts and provides predictable timelines, deliverables, and costs
  • Fabric in action – how the Dept of Transport are using Fabric to address the challenges of disconnected data sources
  • How to get started with Microsoft Fabric including best practices and licensing options
