Having also achieved the esteemed Azure Expert MSP (Managed Service Provider) status, this new Cloud Solutions designation further set us apart as the leading independent Microsoft partner for Ireland and the UK.

Achievement of Microsoft Solutions partner for Cloud is only reached through the acquisition of six distinct Solution Partner designations, showcasing our depth of skills across the Microsoft platform including:

  1. Business Applications (Enterprise Track)
  2. Security
  3. Modern Work
  4. Digital App Innovation (Azure)
  5. Data and AI (Azure): Specialisations in Windows Server & SQL Server Migrations & Analytics on Microsoft Azure
  6. Infrastructure (Azure): Specialisations in Windows Server & SQL Server Migrations & Microsoft Windows Virtual desktop

Reaching these solution designations is based on an evaluation of three criteria: Performance, Skilling, and Customer Success. The evaluation considers a comprehensive range of factors, including customer wins, dedication to skilling and training, and contributions to usage growth.

The most recent success for Codec is the achievement of the Business Applications designation for the Enterprise track. This significant achievement recognises our team's dedication, expertise, and success in specialising in large-scale deployments of Dynamics 365 and Power Platform.

As one of a very small number of independent Microsoft partners in Ireland and the UK to achieve Enterprise status, this designation further sets Codec apart in our reputation as Ireland's leading Microsoft Dynamics partner.

In essence, the Solutions partner designation for Microsoft Cloud underscores Codec's comprehensive skills in cloud services, positioning the company to address diverse client requirements.

Codec aspires to be a long-term, client-centric partner for organisations navigating the expanding landscape of cloud projects.
