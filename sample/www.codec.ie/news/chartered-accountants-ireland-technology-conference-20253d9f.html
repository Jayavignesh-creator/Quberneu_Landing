Chartered Accountants Ireland Technology Conference 2025

Written by Codec | Jan 9, 2025 4:21:28 PM

Codec are delighted to participate as a key sponsor at the Chartered Accountants annual technology conference taking place online on the 17th January. This conference will explore how AI and emerging technologies are transforming the finance and accountancy profession.

With a keynote from Áine Nolan, CFO, Microsoft Ireland on unlocking the value of AI in Finance, there will also be a number of case studies from companies like the Kerry Group and Future Workforce as well sessions around AI in financial reporting and how the role of the accountant is changing for the future. 

ERP practice lead for Codec, David Wyke will deliver a session on Addressing Challenges in Financial Solution Implementations – in which he will offer practical insights, expert advice, and cutting-edge technology demonstrations to help businesses succeed in their Financial Solutions (FinSol) projects.
