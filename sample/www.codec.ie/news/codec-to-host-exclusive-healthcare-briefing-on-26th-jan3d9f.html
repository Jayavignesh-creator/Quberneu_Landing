Codec to host exclusive Healthcare briefing on 26th Jan

Written by Codec | Jan 10, 2023 2:26:25 PM

Codec, in conjunction with Microsoft are excited to host our first healthcare sector focused event for 2023 in the Shelbourne Hotel, Dublin on Thursday, 26th Jan from 8am - 10am. 

Entitled - Digital Transformation in the Healthcare Sector, this exclusive briefing is relevant for senior executives involved in the Healthcare, Pharmaceutical and Life Sciences sectors and will focus on the role of IT in enabling digital transformation.   

With confirmed Microsoft speakers, Frank O'Donnell, Public Sector Director, Ireland, Ciara Perciavalle, Head of Health, Microsoft Ireland, and Rory Cremin, COO of Clanwilliam Group, this 2-hour briefing is not to be missed. 

The briefing will also provide valuable networking opportunities for participants with other healthcare providers as well as access to the most senior Microsoft executives and Codec industry experts.
