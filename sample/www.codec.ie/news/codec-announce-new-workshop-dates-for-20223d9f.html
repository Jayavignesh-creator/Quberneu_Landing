Codec Announce New Workshop Dates for 2022 | Codec Article

Written by Codec | Jan 12, 2022 1:00:00 AM
Codec are pleased to announce that a number of new workshop dates have been added to the calendar for the new year.

Continuing to take place online for the moment, these popular workshops have been instrumental in educating users from a broad range of both public and private sectors in the very latest in Cloud Technologies.

The new workshops planned for the coming months will cover technology areas around Power Apps, Azure Cloud Native, Azure AI as well as Customer Insights.

See below for more details on the planned workshops and their dates as well as links to register your interest in any of these.

  • App in a Day | 3rd February 2022
  • Azure Cloud Native Apps: 16th February 2022
  • Dynamics 365 Customer Insights: 3rd March 2022
  • Azure AI: 7th April 2022

Early booking is advised as these workshops have a limited number of places and get booked up quickly.
