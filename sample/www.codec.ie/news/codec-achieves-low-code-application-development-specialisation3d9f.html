Codec achieves Low Code Application development specialisation

Written by Codec | Mar 26, 2025 4:27:42 PM

Codec, one of the largest specialist Microsoft service partners in Ireland and the UK, has set a new standard for innovation, becoming the first Irish Microsoft partner to earn the Low Code Application Development Specialisation. This specialisation reinforces Codec’s position as a trusted partner for public and private organisations seeking to accelerate their business processes through innovative low-code solutions using the Microsoft Power Platform.

“It’s a fantastic achievement to become the first Irish Microsoft partner to earn the Low Code Application Specialisation” said Ronan Stafford, CEO at Codec. “This recognition highlights Codec’s leadership in delivering transformative digital solutions to organisations across Ireland and the UK. We are proud to collaborate with our clients to address their unique challenges, empower their teams, and deliver bespoke, impactful solutions that drive growth and help them realise their ambitions faster.” 

The Microsoft Low Code Application Development Specialisation is awarded to a select group of partners who demonstrate proven expertise in delivering high-quality, scalable, and efficient low-code solutions. With tools like Microsoft Power Apps, Power Automate, and Microsoft Dataverse, Codec has consistently delivered solutions to clients in the Irish and British public and private sectors that reduce development time, enhance collaboration, and drive innovation for clients.  

Codec earned this advanced specialisation by displaying proven success in implementing and scaling low-code solutions that drive measurable business outcomes. This achievement also underscores the team’s technical excellence, certified expertise in Microsoft Power Platform technologies, and a proven methodology that deploys best practices in app development, workflow automation, and seamless system integration. Achieving a Microsoft Business Application specialization requires a high level of  commitment to excellence due to stringent requirements.

“This recognition highlights Codec’s dedication to empowering businesses with fast, cost-effective application development solutions,” said Andrew Smith, General Manager, Partner Program Management at Microsoft. “As businesses face growing demands for agility and digital transformation, we’re glad to see Codec innovating with customers on  Microsoft solutions  to tackle complex challenges, with speed and user adoption at the forefront.” 
  
Low-code development is transforming how businesses innovate, enabling rapid application delivery and empowering 'citizen developers' to play a key role in digital growth. With this advanced specialisation, Codec solidifies its position as a leader in driving digital transformation for Irish and British businesses. Through secure, scalable low-code solutions that streamline processes, they empower clients to resolve inefficiencies and achieve measurable results more quickly and effectively.

Codec is one of the most experienced and certified Microsoft partners in Ireland and the UK. This specialisation builds on our recent achievement of the AI Platform on Microsoft Azure specialisation. Codec is also one of a select number of partners to achieve the Cloud Solutions badge which is only earned when you have all six Microsoft Solutions Partner designations within the Microsoft Cloud Partner Program.
