Digital Transformation in the Healthcare Sector

Written by Codec | Jan 10, 2023 3:05:56 PM

Digital health is the space where applied technologies can transform healthcare operations.  As home to some of the largest global healthcare-tech organisations in the world, Ireland is well positioned to be a global leader in the digital health space.

To achieve these innovations, though, healthcare and life science organisations must be able to leverage data-driven efficiencies and insights. This strategic advantage can only be achieved when data is properly digitised, consolidated, and analysed.

We are pleased to offer a strategic Healthcare briefing targeting senior executives interested in learning more about Digital Transformation in the sector and the role of IT in enabling the transformation. 

 When: Thursday 26th Jan 2023

Time: 8am - 10am

Where: Shelbourne Hotel, Dublin

Who: Senior executives in the Health and Life sciences sectors. 

With confirmed Microsoft speakers, Frank O'Donnell, Public Sector Director, Ireland, Ciara Perciavalle, Head of Health, Microsoft Ireland, and Rory Cremin, COO of Clanwilliam Group, this 2-hour briefing is not to be missed. 

Event agenda:

  • 8am - Registration, Breakfast & Networking (Stevens suite, Shelbourne Hotel)

  • 8.30am - Digital transformation in the healthcare sector (Microsoft)

  • 9am - Connecting your data to get closer to your patients (Clanwilliam Group)

  • 9.30am - Impacting health outcomes (Codec)

  • 9.45am - Panel discussion & Q&A

The briefing will also provide valuable networking opportunities for participants with other healthcare providers as well as access to the most senior Microsoft executives and Codec industry experts.
