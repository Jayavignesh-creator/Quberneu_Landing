Codec launch new brand identity

Written by Codec | Sep 7, 2022 3:02:10 PM

We are proud and excited to announce the launch of the new Codec brand.

Our new position in the market can be succinctly summarised in two words, “Impact, delivered.”

For over 35 years, we have evolved to meet the needs of an ever-changing market and the only constant has been our reputation to deliver impact and add value to our clients. We have grown in scale over the years, with our team currently numbering over 250.

In a world of uncertainty, we back up these words with action. We do the right thing. We do the basics brilliantly. We care, personally. We take the risk out of IT transformation by adopting a proven framework for delivery. Indeed, we obsess about delivery. We get new technologies working for our clients, the first time around. In an industry riddled with nervous apprehension, we consistently deliver 95% of our projects on budget.

Today, we are the only Microsoft Gold Cloud Business Applications Certified Partner in Ireland and one of only eighty-three Azure Expert Partners in the world.

To better reflect our values and our new positioning in the market, the decision was made over a year ago to transition a new brand identity. To this end, we engaged the services of a 3rd party agency, Squaredot to work with us in the re-development of our www.codec.ie and www.codec.uk websites.

On our new websites you will notice that our visual identity has changed, including a new logo, colour palette, sales collateral, and marketing content.

Rest assured that everything that got us to this point will never change, as we continue to evolve, to innovate, to add value and deliver tangible business impact with our growing list of long-term partnerships.

We hope you like our new brand identity and we welcome you to explore our new website .
