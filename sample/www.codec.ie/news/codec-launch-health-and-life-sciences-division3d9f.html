Codec launch Health and Life Sciences Division

Written by Codec | Nov 2, 2022 9:16:16 AM

Codec are pleased to announce the creation of a new Health and Life Sciences division.

This division, headed up by Michelle McKenna, Health & Life Sciences Account Director, is dedicated to serving the fast-growing needs of the healthcare sector in Ireland and the UK.

Digital transformation is driving better patient outcomes within the Irish Healthcare sector. This indigenous growth, combined with significant inward investment over many years in the medical and pharmaceutical sectors, have combined to create a dynamic and growing health and life sciences industry.

Codec, delivering for more than 35 years in the Irish market, have worked with some of Ireland's biggest and most successful healthcare companies. With the creation of this new division, we are consolidating our learned experiences to create a team that understand sector specific challenges and focus on the delivery of solutions to meet those challenges head on.

Michelle, who heads up this division, joins Codec with more than 20 years proven international consultative experience in strategic leadership, customer success, delivery management and business development in the health sector. She is experienced in leading customers, teams and partners through digital transformation and change management projects, while building valued key stakeholder relationships.

In the creation of this dedicated division, Codec recognise that a "one size fits all" approach does not work for all industries and that combining learnings and experiences from our client engagements allow us to offer sector specific solutions that will ultimately drive better health outcomes, improve security, and allow greater data operability and scalability.

Codec are offering services for the health & life sciences sector around the Microsoft technology platform for every stage in your digital transformation journey.
