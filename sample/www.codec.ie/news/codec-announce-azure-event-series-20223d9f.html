Codec Announce Azure Event Series 2022

Written by Codec | Sep 7, 2022 3:12:12 PM

Codec are delighted to announce the launch of our Business transformation Azure event series.

This series will incorporate 3 x in-person events over the Autumn, around key Azure cloud technologies and how they can impact on business transformation, namely: Business continuity and disaster recovery, data estate modernisation, and application modernisation.

Taking place in Sept, Oct, and Nov respectively, these events will be jointly hosted in conjunction with Microsoft Ireland and will take place at their impressive head office location, One Microsoft place, Leopardstown, D.18.

Organisations today are challenged with delivering business transformation across the business and Cloud technologies are seen as a key enabler in meeting many of the challenges.

Codec, a Microsoft Gold partner, and winner of Azure partner of the year 2022 for Azure infrastructure and data and AI are hosting this series of workshops around the Azure platform to help organisations understand how the Azure cloud platform can transform their business.
