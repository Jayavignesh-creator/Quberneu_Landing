Codec to Sponsor Belfast Telegraph IT Awards | Codec Article

Written by Codec | Oct 5, 2021 12:00:00 AM

The Belfast Telegraph IT Awards recognise and celebrate the IT successes of individuals and organisations across Northern Ireland. The awards attract entries from across all sectors and sizes of businesses and culminate in an awards ceremony on the 5th Nov hosted in the MAC Belfast.

Codec are delighted to participate once again as sponsor of “Digital Project of the Year” category. This award recognises the contribution of companies who prove innovation in both the use of technology and business improvement.

The awards are judged by an expert panel of impartial judges that bring with them decades of IT acumen and experience.

Entries in each category will be judged against three key criteria:

  • Innovation
  • Measurable Success
  • Customer Satisfaction

Closing date for entries is 11th October 2021.

For a full list of categories and to enter your chosen award category, click here.

Codec are proud to support these worthwhile awards by sponsoring this category and we look forward to a great evening on the 5th Nov in the MAC Belfast.
