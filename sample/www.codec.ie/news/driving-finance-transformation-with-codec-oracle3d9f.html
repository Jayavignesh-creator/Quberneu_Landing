Driving finance transformation with Codec & Oracle: 21st April 2023 

In 2023, CFOs will continue to drive Finance Transformation while setting their organisations up to navigate the recession and drive profitability (source: Gartner). Additionally, retaining top talent is a challenge faced by many organisations. Finance and HR need to be aligned in order to build the workforce needed to execute on strategic organisational goals.

Join Codec and our partner Oracle for a dedicated Oracle breakfast briefing around driving finance transformation and the strategic alignment of finance and HR in strategic workforce planning for the future.  

When? Friday, 21st April 2023
Time? 8.30am - 10.15am
Where? Shelbourne Hotel, Dublin 

This breakfast briefing taking place in the Shelbourne hotel is specifically targeted at existing users of Oracle software (or those who are considering Oracle solutions). This is a great opportunity to meet with key Oracle and Codec executives and to learn more about the latest product developments and future roadmaps for Oracle technologies. 

See the technology in action with a live demo of workforce planning on the day and hear from an Oracle customer on how using the solution is helping them to drive efficiencies and cost savings across the business. 

We look forward to seeing you there on the day!
