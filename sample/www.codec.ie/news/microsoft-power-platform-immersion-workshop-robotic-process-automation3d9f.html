Microsoft Power Platform immersion workshop: robotic process automation (RPA): 5th May 2022

Written by Codec | Apr 19, 2022 12:00:00 AM
5th of May 2022 | 10:00 AM – 02:30 PM | (GMT+00:00) Dublin, Edinburgh, Lisbon, London

Join Codec and Microsoft to learn how to build intelligent automation solutions with Power Automate at Microsoft Power Platform Immersion Workshop: Robotic Process Automation. You’ll get hands-on experience with cloud flows, desktop flows and form processing in AI Builder. And, learn how to provide invoice approvals through a human in the loop pattern in Microsoft Teams.

Who should attend:

  • IT professionals
  • Developers

Technical level:

  • Attendees should have a basic understanding of the fundamental scope of the technology and an opportunity to integrate without practice or hands-on experience.
  • Not sure if this workshop is right for you? To better understand the technical depth of the content that will be presented, check out the class materials.

After completing this workshop, you will:

  • Have hands-on experience building your own low-code solutions.
  • Learn how to automate processes across your business.
  • Find out how to enhance automation technology with intelligence and human-based interaction.

Here’s what you can expect:

Workshop Presentation and Discussion Instructor Led Experience
Introduction Hands-on Lab: How to generate insights
  Hands-on Lab: Create your first Power Automate desktop flow
  Hands-on Lab: Use input & output parameters
  Hands-on Lab: Use desktop flow in a cloud flow – gateway
  Break: 10 minutes
  Hands-on Lab: Use Outlook email
  Hands-on Lab: Integrate with Microsoft Teams
  Hands-on Lab: Automate web scenario
  Closing and Next Steps
