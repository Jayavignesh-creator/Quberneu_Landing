Codec to Sponsor Digital DNA Awards -30 March 2023

Written by Codec | Mar 7, 2023 11:18:47 AM

Codec UK is pleased to sponsor the “E-Commerce Project” category at the forthcoming Digital DNA Awards, being hosted in Belfast ICC on the 30th of March 2023. The awards, which were established in 2016, celebrate the achievements of individuals, projects and companies in Northern Ireland's highly competitive tech and digital sector. 

With 16 categories available, the Digital DNA Awards are now widely regarded as the biggest night of the year for the Northern Ireland Tech Community. The 16 categories focus on achievements on a Project, Company and Individual level.  

Codec, which works with clients to develop and achieve their digital transformation goals, is excited to be a part of these important awards. The company's sector-specific insights, customisable services and specialised tools enable enterprise leaders to maximise the potential of digital transformation.

As a sponsor of the Digital DNA Awards, Codec aims to support and celebrate the success of individuals, projects and companies in the tech and digital community in Northern Ireland. Codec recognises the importance of recognising and showcasing excellence in this dynamic and constantly evolving industry. 

The Digital DNA Awards are independently judged and free to enter. The winners of each category will be announced on 30 March 2023 at the Belfast ICC.  

We look forward to a great evening and we wish all the finalists in all the categories the very best of luck on the night.
