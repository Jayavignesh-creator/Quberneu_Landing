Codec to Partner with Solver, a Global Leader in Corporate Performance Management

Written by Codec | May 3, 2022 4:00:00 AM

Codec are delighted to announce our strategic partnership with Solver, a global leader in cloud and web-based Corporate Performance Management (CPM) for multiple ERP systems.

This partnership allows Codec to market and re-sell Solver’s cloud-based business planning and reporting software to the Irish Market.

Solver is redefining the category of cloud-based business planning and reporting software with ground-breaking technology that empowers teams with customised insights.

The Solver solution combines financial and other key data into a single tool, powered by the most flexible report and planning form designer on the market. Organisations use this solution to automate and streamline financial and operational reports, consolidations, and budgeting and forecasting processes – as well as ‘What If’ planning that helps drive confident decisions during unprecedented times.

John Roddy, Commercial Director with Codec said, “We are very excited about this partnership with Solver.  Codec has a strong heritage in delivering technology solutions for over 30 years in the Enterprise Performance Management and Dynamics ERP space working with vendors including Microsoft, Oracle, Anaplan and Infor.  The Solver solution complements our offerings perfectly and we’re confident that it will bring significant added value to our customers in enabling them to make faster and better business decisions.”

Commenting on the partnership, Gavin Duffy, Managing Director for Solver Ireland said, “Codec is a great fit for us – not only because they are one of Ireland’s biggest players in the EPM space but also because they are a Microsoft Gold partner specialising in Dynamics ERP solutions. They bring a wealth of skills, expertise and local market knowledge to the table and we are very excited about the opportunities that lie ahead for us both” he added.
