Codec achieves Microsoft Threat Protection specialisation

Written by Codec | Jan 16, 2025 5:14:29 PM

As cyber threats become increasingly sophisticated, organisations need security partners they can trust. That's why we're proud to announce that Codec has achieved Microsoft's Threat Protection specialisation certification – a validation of our proven ability to protect enterprises against advanced security threats.

"I'm very proud of our team for obtaining this certificate,” said Codec CEO Ronan Stafford. “The constant evolution of technology means that companies need more than just traditional security measures to maintain digital security. This specialisation represents our dedication to excellence in cybersecurity. We wouldn’t have accomplished this without proven client success in this field, and it’s fantastic that this success is now recognised and certified.”

This certification follows two key milestones in cloud security achieved in 2024 – receiving Microsoft's Cloud Security Specialisation and the recognition of our Cloud Security Team Lead, Tushar Kumar, as a Microsoft Most Valued Professional in Azure Networking Infrastructure and Security. These achievements underscore our expertise in delivering enterprise-grade security solutions that protect our clients' digital assets.

Receiving the Threat Specialisation certification validates our expertise in implementing and managing Microsoft's comprehensive security ecosystem, including Microsoft Threat Protection, Microsoft Cloud App Security, and Azure Sentinel. The certification process required our team to demonstrate deep technical knowledge and practical expertise in implementing Microsoft's security solutions across diverse enterprise environments. This comprehensive validation ensures our clients receive best-in-class security solutions that are effective against data loss, cloud app security, and advanced threat detection.

Tushar Kumar, Security Team Lead at Codec and a Microsoft MVP for Azure Infrastructure and Security explained the technical significance behind this certification: "The Microsoft Threat Protection specialisation goes beyond basic security implementation. It validates our ability to architect and deploy integrated security solutions that provide end-to-end protection across cloud and hybrid environments. This means we can offer our clients enhanced threat detection, automated response capabilities, and comprehensive security management through Microsoft's advanced toolset."

Whether you're looking to strengthen your existing security infrastructure or build a new security framework from the ground up, our Microsoft-certified team can help. We'll work with you to assess your security needs and deploy protection that grows with your business.
