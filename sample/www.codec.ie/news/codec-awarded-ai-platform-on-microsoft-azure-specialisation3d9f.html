Codec awarded AI Platform on Microsoft Azure specialisation

Written by Codec | Mar 12, 2025 12:02:21 PM

In recognition of our achievements in the field of AI model development and integration, we have achieved the AI Platform on Microsoft Azure specialisation. This reflects our proven success in designing and deploying AI solutions for our customers that enhance their process efficiency and drive business growth. 

“Achieving this specialisation is a fantastic milestone for us.” highlighted Codec’s Azure Practice Director, Stephen Black. “Being one of the elite number of Irish Microsoft partners to obtain this recognition reinforces our position as a leader of AI-driven solutions in Ireland. It reflects the exceptional level of knowledge and dedication of our team to deliver real impact to our clients.” 

Earning the AI Platform on Microsoft Azure specialisation validates our ability to design, implement, and manage AI solutions that integrate seamlessly with Microsoft’s cloud ecosystem. The certification process required us to demonstrate our proficiency in developing and deploying AI models using Azure Machine Learning, Cognitive Services, and AI-powered analytics tools. This specialisation certifies that our clients receive advanced AI solutions suited to their business needs. 

This certification is the latest in a series of Microsoft recognitions we’ve received in recent months and adds to our extensive list of specialisations including our Analytics in Azure Specialisation which validates our ability to design, implement, and manage Data Solutions in Azure, including Microsoft Fabric. Adding the AI Platform on Microsoft Azure specialisation to this list further strengthens Codec’s position as a trusted partner in digital transformation, helping businesses innovate and grow thanks to their enhanced technology capabilities. Other recent certifications include Cloud Security, Threat Protection, and Low Code Application specialisations.  

"This certification validates our experience in implementing AI solutions that leverage Azure’s secure, scalable, and flexible platform,” explained Alberto Merinos, Data & AI Practice Lead at Codec. "With Azure’s AI services, we can help businesses develop, train, and deploy machine learning models using tools like Azure Cognitive Services, Azure Machine Learning, and frameworks such as TensorFlow and PyTorch. As a result, our customers will be able to integrate AI into their Azure system with confidence, knowing that they have a partner with the skills and experience to support them as they advance their AI journey.”

We help businesses integrate AI into their Azure environments, ensuring a smooth deployment and real business impact. Each solution is designed to align with business objectives, scale with growth, and enhance decision-making across operations.
