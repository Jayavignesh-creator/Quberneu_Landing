Unlocking the benefits of AI for Start-ups

Written by Codec | Apr 5, 2023 9:44:15 AM

The use of AI technology is a hot topic. There is a lack of understanding around the potential of the technology and many may not recognise the true benefits and possibilities that implementing AI services can bring to their organisation.

Codec are pleased to host this information evening which will seek to demystify the concept of AI services and how they can benefit your organisation as it grows. We will be joined on the evening by Microsoft as well as Swoop funding both of whom will be able to advise not only on an appropriate technology journey for your company but also how to finance your initiatives. 

This event which will be informal in style will be an invaluable source of information and provide excellent networking opportunities for the Start-up community. The session will start at 5.30pm and will finish up at about 7pm for networking and some food and drinks.

We do hope you will join us at the event. You will be in great company with others who are passionate about the startup ecosystem. 

Event details:

5:30pm - Arrival & registration

6pm - Introduction, and speakers

7pm - Networking, food & drinks
