Codec achieve Cloud Security Specialisation

Written by Codec | Apr 4, 2024 4:32:56 PM

Codec are delighted to announce our achievement of the Cloud Security Specialisation from Microsoft.  This is a momentous achievement and one that reflects our commitment to providing our customers with the highest level of security solutions. 

Achievement of this specialisation demonstrates that we have the skills and capabilities in implementing comprehensive security solutions across Azure, hybrid and multi-cloud environments to the highest levels.  Our expertise was verified through a rigorous audit process which involved a number of Microsoft exams and customer references where we have demonstrated the ability to deploy and manage these workloads. 

The accomplishment comes on the back of our recent announcement of Most Valuable Professional (MVP)  within the Azure Networking Infrastructure and Security technology area for our lead Azure security consultant, Tushar Kumar. 

Codec are committed to providing our customers with the highest levels of skills and expertise on the Microsoft platform.  As a long standing Microsoft partner for over 3 decades, Codec are also the current Microsoft Partner of the Year for Ireland and we recently announced our achievement of the Cloud solutions badge which can only be achieved once you have certified for 6 unique solution designations. 

This new Cloud Security Specialisation further set us apart as the leading independent Microsoft partner for Ireland and the UK.
