Codec scoop 3 awards at Microsoft Partner of the Year ceremony 2023

Written by Codec | Jul 25, 2023 3:21:47 PM

Last Thursday at the local Microsoft awards ceremony held at the Dublin Royal Convention Centre, Codec received not one, but three prestigious awards!

First and foremost, we are proud to have been officially named Microsoft Country Partner of the Year 2023, a recognition of our dedication and commitment to excellence.

Additionally, we are honored to have received two more awards: Business Applications Partner of the Year 2023 and Driving New Business Partner of the Year 2023 and we were a finalist for the Azure Data & AI Partner of the Year.  

Business Applications Partner of Year recognizes partners who have excelled in delivering solutions around Dynamics 365 for their customers.  This is the 8th year in a row Codec have achieved this award and it makes it 11 times in total that we have achieved this award. No other partner in Ireland has the skills, experience or the accolades in D365 implementations and migrations and we are incredibly proud of our achievements yet again in providing first class solutions to our customers in Ireland and the UK. 

Driving New Business Partner of the Year is a new award for 2023 and recognizes the partner that have used new and innovative sales and marketing processes to drive growth and acquire new customers. This award highlights the partner's ability to think outside the box and implement creative strategies that have proven successful in capturing new business opportunities.

At Codec, we take great pride in our innovative approach to sales and marketing. We have continuously explored new avenues and embraced cutting-edge technologies to reach our target audience and showcase the value of our solutions. Our team has invested significant time and effort in researching the latest trends and understanding the evolving needs of our customers, allowing us to tailor our strategies accordingly.
